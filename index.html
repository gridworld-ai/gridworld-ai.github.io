About Us

Gridworld is an applied research company on a mission to bring forth self-optimizing autonomous factories. We believe that factories of the future will be run completely by AI agents, delivering superhuman performance on throughput, safety, and sustainability.

We are first tackling thermal processes such as industrial dryers, boilers, and furnaces, where thermal lag and delayed feedback means that performance can be significantly improved with RL-driven long-horizon optimization.

Our Approach

High-Fidelity Environments

Accurate models of your industrial processes, capturing all relevant process variables and interactions.

SOP Cloning

Neural networks that serve as behavioral clones (BC) of your best operators and industrial engineers.

Our Technology

High-Fidelity Environments

Our environments build on top of digital twin formats, and are purpose-built for AI training. They're computationally efficient, enabling accelerated simulated experiments necessary for AI training.

Reinforcement Learning

Reinforcement learning is an AI paradigm where intelligent agents learn optimal strategies beyond human intuition that outperform decades of hand-tuned controllers through iterative loop-based training.
