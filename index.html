About the Company

From RL to Finance

Gridworld Holdings Ltd, or Gridworld, was started by two Oxford graduates as a technology startup making high-quality reinforcement learning (RL) environments for simulation-based AI training.

The company is now a proprietary firm, operating two funds: Gamma-0 and Gamma-1. Gridworld leverages its technology expertise to develop AI/ML tools for both investing and trading.

Funds

Gamma-0

The Gamma-0 fund is Gridworld's quantitative trading operation. It focuses on algorithmically exploiting market inefficiencies on timescales longer than typical quantitative trading firms.

Gamma-1

The Gamma-1 fund is Gridworld's discretionary fund. Technology plays a more advisory role here than in the Gamma-0 fund; our AI/ML tools provide critical information to support investment and trading theses, and the actual investments and trades are made on a discretionary basis.
