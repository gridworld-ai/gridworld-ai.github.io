About

At Gridworld, we provide research services through reinforcement learning (RL), a machine learning paradigm in which intelligent strategies arise from interactions with a digital simulation of the problem (the 'environment').

Our approach is a 3-step process: (1) create high fidelity environment(s) for your project, (2) design rewards to meet your objectives, and (3) extract and sense-make solutions from the resultant RL-trained AI models.

We serve a wide variety of sectors ranging from manufacturing to financial services, working closely with our clients to deeply understand each unique problem space.
