We are team from Oxford providing end-to-end Reinforcement Learning (RL) solutions for the manufacturing industry. Manufacturing can be seen as a highly complex "game" that has been played at human level since its inception. Our belief is that the AlphaGo moment for manufacturing is coming, where AI develops even better manufacturing strategies that can be efficiently deployed once discovered. We aim to arrive at this moment by developing high-fidelity simulation models of manufacturing processes and then training RL agents to optimize them for given objectives.

Our initial focus is on optimizing temperature control in physical manufacturing processes, involving thermal physics and material modelling. We work with our customers to develop bespoke temperature control simulations and then train RL to discover optimal strategies for them that can be deployed without the need for additional hardware or robotics. We aim to expand to other processes in the future, creating a suite of simulation tools that can cover a wide range of manufacturing domains.
