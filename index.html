Our Technology

We combine two powerful technologies to transform industrial process control

Digital Twins

A digital twin is a high-fidelity virtual replica of your physical industrial system—capturing thermal dynamics, material properties, and process behavior with precision.

Our Edge

Unlike traditional digital twins built for monitoring, ours are purpose-built for AI training. They're computationally efficient, enabling millions of simulated experiments that would be impossible on real hardware.

  • Risk-free experimentation at scale
  • Optimized for rapid RL iteration
  • Validated against real-world data

Reinforcement Learning

Reinforcement learning is an AI paradigm where intelligent agents learn optimal control strategies through trial and error—receiving rewards for actions that improve outcomes.

Why It Matters

RL agents can discover non-intuitive strategies that outperform decades of hand-tuned controllers. They continuously adapt, finding optimal trade-offs between efficiency, quality, and throughput.

  • Learns beyond human intuition
  • Handles complex, dynamic systems
  • Improves with real-world feedback

The Synergy

Our efficient digital twins enable RL agents to train on millions of scenarios in hours—exploring edge cases, failure modes, and optimization opportunities that would take years to encounter in the real world. The result: AI controllers that are battle-tested before they ever touch your production line.
