About

Human-Machine Interfaces (HMIs) are the gateway to industrial monitoring and control, displaying real-time data and alerts from SCADA servers which site operators rely on to make critical decisions.

Gridworld is building AI-native HMIs, imbuing them with greater agency and intelligence with our context-aware AI copilots. We deliver superior decision support and democratize your best operators' knowledge and experience, ensuring your sites are always running at peak performance.

Connectivity
Universal Protocol Support

Native drivers for Modbus RTU/TCP, OPC-UA, Siemens S7, BACnet IP, MQTT, Ethernet/IP (Allen Bradley), and WebAPI. Connect any device on your plant floor without bespoke integration work.

Visualization
Real-Time Process Views

Web-based SCADA/HMI editor with drag-and-drop design. Build custom dashboards, P&ID diagrams, animated pipe flows, and live-data widgets entirely in the browser, no client software required.

Alarming
Threshold Alerts & Notifications

Define thresholds, trigger conditions, and multi-channel notifications. AI triage surfaces the alerts that actually need operator attention.

Historian
DAQ & Historical Trending

Built-in data acquisition stores tag histories. Chart controls render time-series with configurable interpolation. AI surfacing highlights anomalies before they cascade into downtime.

Automation
Scheduler & Scripting Engine

Built-in scheduler and scripting layer enable time-based or event-driven automation. Node-RED integration extends to any IIoT workflow without leaving the FUXA environment.

Platform
Cross-Platform & Edge-Ready

Full-stack Node.js backend with an Angular/HTML5/SVG frontend. Runs on Windows, Linux, macOS, Raspberry Pi, and Docker. Deploy at the edge or in your cloud.
