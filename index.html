The AlphaGo moment for manufacturing is coming, where AI discovers better strategies than even the best industrial engineers. This will be achieved through a combination of high-fidelity simulation models of manufacturing processes and Reinforcement Learning (RL) techniques.

We are a team from Oxford providing end-to-end Reinforcement Learning (RL) solutions for the manufacturing industry, with a focus on optimizing temperature control in physical manufacturing processes. We work with our customers to develop bespoke temperature control simulations and then train RL to discover optimal strategies for them that can be deployed without the need for additional hardware or robotics. We aim to expand to other processes in the future, creating a suite of simulation tools to cover a wider range of manufacturing domains.
