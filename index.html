About the Company

From RL to Finance

Gridworld Holdings Ltd, or Gridworld, was started by two graduates from the University of Oxford initially as an AI company making environments for reinforcement learning (RL). The company now operates as a proprietary firm, using our technology expertise to develop AI/ML tools for quantamental analysis, covering decision-support across all layers (individual securities, sector, macro) of the market.

Despite the heavy focus on technology, Gridworld's thesis formation process is largely discretionary, as we believe that the best outcomes are generated through deep collaboration between man and machine.
