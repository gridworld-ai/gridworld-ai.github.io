About Us

Human Machine Interfaces (HMIs) are the gateway to industrial monitoring and control, displaying real-time data and alerts from SCADA servers which site operators rely on to make critical decisions.

Gridworld is building AI-powered HMIs, imbuing them with greater agency and intelligence with our context-aware AI copilots. They deliver superior decision support and democratize your best operators' knowledge and experience, ensuring your sites are always running at peak performance.
